About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to both preserving and sharing the legacy of the Norwegian Trolls. Our mission is to help spread awareness of stories that many thought were only legends. The Tobias Foundation was named in honor of Tobias Tidemann, who was instrumental in letting us know that these creatures were not just mere folklore, but rather physical beings that still live in our world.

On July 31st, 2021 the planet Venus aligned with Saturn and Pluto in a rare planetary alignment. The gravitational pull from this alignment resulted in the polar ice caps melting over 100 tons of ice in less than 30 days and ultimately led to the discovery of a new species, the Norwegian Troll.

"In order to protect the Norwegian Trolls, we must continue to fund scientific research. Your donation will help us learn more about these magical creatures and how we can best protect them. " - Dr.Tidemann
